About Us

Vinodh Balu: Known as "Blood Man"

Vinodh Balu, widely recognized as "Blood Man", is a passionate advocate for blood donation and community wellness. With over a decade of experience, he has orchestrated and led more than 950 blood donation camps, resulting in the collection of over 50,000 life-saving blood units.

His dedication to raising awareness about the importance of blood donation has seen him deliver impactful speeches on various stages, inspiring countless individuals and organizations to contribute to this noble cause.

Key Achievements:

  • ✔ Orchestrated and managed 950+ blood donation camps across diverse communities.
  • ✔ Successfully collected over 50,000 blood units, significantly alleviating blood shortages.
  • ✔ Delivered compelling speeches on numerous platforms, promoting the culture of blood donation.
  • ✔ Fostered partnerships with corporations, NGOs, and government organizations.
